Curseforge v2.1.0 Mermaids Description.

### <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Ever wanted to be a Mermaid? Now you can with Mermaids, this mod allows the player to <span style="color: rgb(53, 152, 219);">transform into a Mermaid</span> when entering the water! Which will give the player increased movement speed, water breathing, night vision, and more while swimming underwater throughout the seas, oceans, and rivers.</span>
 

<span style="color: #236fa1;">===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===</span>

## <span style="color: #3598db;"><strong>Features</strong>:</span>

<span style="color: #c2e0f4;">After entering the water for a couple seconds, you'll turn into a mermaid. Once you enter the land, your legs will regenerate (Can be modified).</span>

*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Grow a Mermaid Tail and keep your cosmetics.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Breathe underwater with increased swim speed.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Mod fully customizable with permissions and config.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Configurable transformation option -- Example: Transform only by potions and/or enable for all.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Custom model, cosmetics, and animations, WIP.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Compatibility support with 9+ mod. Includes custom Mermaids mod API.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">Works in Singleplayer and Multiplayer.</span>
*   <span style="color: #c2e0f4;">and More!</span>

<span style="color: #236fa1;">===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===--===</span>
